About Me

I'm Abis Paudel, a 23-year-old civil engineering graduate ready to embark on an impactful career. My journey began with a strong educational grounding at Little Angels' School Hattiban and Little Angels' College Hattiban, where I cultivated a passion for learning and problem-solving. I then refined my technical prowess at Advanced College of Engineering and Management, proudly earning my Bachelor's degree in civil engineering. As a graduate from the 2077 batch, passing out in 2081, I'm now poised to apply my knowledge and contribute to innovative projects that will help shape our Nation.

Work

My journey in Civil Engineering is defined by a rigorous blend of academic achievement, hands-on practical application, and strategic industry involvement. I am passionate about contributing to robust infrastructure development, driven by a keen interest in continuous learning and innovation.

Structural Analysis & Design

My academic rigor culminated in my final year project, where I gained invaluable experience in the structural analysis and design of buildings using ETABS. This intensive engagement provided a solid understanding of complex load behaviors, structural optimization, and the critical importance of adhering to stringent engineering codes and standards.

Practical Application & Software Proficiency (CEPAD Hydro Consultants Pvt.Ltd)

My professional capabilities were significantly enhanced through practical exposure at CEPAD Hydro Consultants Pvt.Ltd. Here, I developed proficiency in essential civil engineering software and methodologies, including:

  • AutoCAD & Civil 3D: Gaining hands-on experience in precise drafting, site layout, and various civil works.
  • Topographical & Alignment Design: Mastering the creation of detailed contours and road alignments, fundamental for accurate project planning.
  • Earthwork & Hydraulic Design: Performing critical road cut/fill calculations and designing intricate anchor blocks for hydraulic structures.
  • Flood Level Analysis (HEC-RAS): Acquiring expertise in hydrological modeling to conduct comprehensive flood level analysis using HEC-RAS, vital for effective water resource management and risk mitigation.

Strategic Leadership & Industry Involvement

Beyond technical execution, my roles in the energy sector underscore my broader understanding of project lifecycle and strategic development:

  • Former Director at Richet Jalbidhyut Company Limited: Gained insights into the operational and developmental aspects of hydropower projects.
  • Current Director at Mount Everest Power Development Limited: Actively contributing to the strategic direction and growth of power development initiatives.
  • Current Director at HydroConnection Pvt.Ltd: Continuing my involvement in the vital hydropower sector, focusing on industry connections and project facilitation.

Commitment to Continuous Growth

My dedication to staying at the forefront of engineering innovation is unwavering. I am currently expanding my advanced analytical toolkit by undergoing training in building structural analysis using STAAD.Pro, ensuring my skills remain contemporary and ready for the most complex engineering challenges. My keen interest in learning more drives my pursuit of excellence in every endeavor.
